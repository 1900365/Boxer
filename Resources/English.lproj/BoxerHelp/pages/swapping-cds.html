Swapping CDs in multi-CD games

  1. Press Cmd ⌘+I to bring up the Drives Inspector.
  2. Select the current CD-ROM and eject it, by dragging it out of the window or clicking the button.
    • If you have a physical CD: insert it into your Mac’s CD/DVD drive and it will be added automatically to DOS.
    • If you have a folder or disc image: drag it into the Drives panel to add it to DOS.
