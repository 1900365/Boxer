Decorating your games

Nostalgia doesn’t have to stop at playing your games. Why not make your collection into a shrine to your misspent youth?

Making purty cover-art icons

Boxer lets you turn game cover scans (or any image you like) into great hi-res icons for your gameboxes. Just find an image you want to use, then either:

Drop your image onto the game icon once you’ve finished importing a game; or

  1. Launch a gamebox,
  2. open the Game tab of the Inspector,
  3. drop your image onto the dropzone.

If you use an opaque image (e.g. a JPG), then Boxer will apply a shiny box effect to the icon.
If you use an image with transparency data (e.g. a 32-bit PNG), then Boxer will use the image as-is.

Et voilà! Soon you’ll have a shelf full of gorgeous games.

Where can I find cover art to use?

  • Mobygames has a very comprehensive selection, though images are watermarked.
  • Abandonia has box scans for much of their collection.
  • Wikipedia has retouched and watermark-free box images for many games.
